## Medical Reasoning Fine-Tuned Models (GPT-OSS-20B based)

1. **Medical Reasoning GPT-OSS-20B** ([dousery/medical-reasoning-gpt-oss-20b](https://huggingface.co/dousery/medical-reasoning-gpt-oss-20b))
   - Fine-tuned version of openai/gpt-oss-20b specifically optimized for medical reasoning and clinical decision-making.

2. **Tonic/med-gpt-oss-20b** ([Tonic/med-gpt-oss-20b](https://huggingface.co/Tonic/med-gpt-oss-20b))
   - Fine-tuned version of OpenAI's GPT-OSS-20B model for medical reasoning and instruction following.

3. **blanar/gpt-oss-20b-medical-reasoner** ([blanar/gpt-oss-20b-medical-reasoner](https://huggingface.co/blanar/gpt-oss-20b-medical-reasoner))
   - Fine-tuned on the FreedomIntelligence/medical-o1-reasoning-SFT dataset.
   - Variants:
     - [blanar/gpt-oss-20b-medical-reasoner-2](https://huggingface.co/blanar/gpt-oss-20b-medical-reasoner-2)
     - [blanar/gpt-oss-20b-medical-reasoner-o1](https://huggingface.co/blanar/gpt-oss-20b-medical-reasoner-o1)

4. **ericrisco/gpt-oss-20b-medical-rescue** ([ericrisco/gpt-oss-20b-medical-rescue](https://huggingface.co/ericrisco/gpt-oss-20b-medical-rescue))
   - ~20B parameter model fine-tuned with Unsloth on 80k+ Q&A dataset focused on first aid, emergency medicine, and clinical reasoning.

5. **MehdiHosseiniMoghadam/Bio-gpt-oss-20B** ([MehdiHosseiniMoghadam/Bio-gpt-oss-20B](https://huggingface.co/MehdiHosseiniMoghadam/Bio-gpt-oss-20B))
   - Specialized biomedical question-answering model fine-tuned on scholarly Q&A extraction tasks from biomedical research papers.

6. **Sandipan1976/gpt-oss-20b-medical-chat-reasoner** ([Sandipan1976/gpt-oss-20b-medical-chat-reasoner](https://huggingface.co/Sandipan1976/gpt-oss-20b-medical-chat-reasoner))
   - Fine-tuned on the HuggingFaceH4/medical-reasoner-oss dataset.
   - Variant: [Sandipan1976/gpt-oss-medical](https://huggingface.co/Sandipan1976/gpt-oss-medical)

7. **kingabzpro/gpt-oss-20b-medical-qa** ([kingabzpro/gpt-oss-20b-medical-qa](https://huggingface.co/kingabzpro/gpt-oss-20b-medical-qa))
   - Fine-tuned on the FreedomIntelligence/medical-o1-verifiable-problem dataset.

8. **sayannath/gpt-oss-20b-medical-qa** ([sayannath/gpt-oss-20b-medical-qa](https://huggingface.co/sayannath/gpt-oss-20b-medical-qa))
